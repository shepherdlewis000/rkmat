CHICAGO BRICK MASONRY

Quality brick construction & repair for Chicago & Northwest Indiana.

Brick construction and repair executed with precision!

Brick is one of the most durable building materials in use! It is both naturally insect & moisture repellant, and helps reduce mold and pests more than wood or other building materials. It's a fire-resistant material that can withstand the most extreme of Chicago weather & winds!


RKMAT has more than 20 years experience working with brick. Whether it's new construction or or maintenance on older brick structures, we have the know-how and experience!

BRICK SERVICES WE OFFER

RK Masonry and Tuckpointing provides Chicago area and Northwest Indiana with repair and restoration services for any existing brick structures or accents you may have. We can create any brick structure, accent, finish, or new construction you need for your next project - on time and on budget!

OUR PASSION IS TO BRING YOUR BRICK PROJECTS TO LIFE!

Our work includes outdoor and indoor brick fireplaces, brick porches, brick siding and facades, brick chimneys, brick retaining walls, brick patios and much, much more.
